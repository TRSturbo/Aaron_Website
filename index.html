Welcome! I am Aaron Tharpe.

Professional About Me

Experienced Engineering Manager and seasoned developer with a passion for building impactful web and mobile experiences. Over a year and a half as an Engineering Manager, and more than three years in team leadership roles, I've had the privilege of leading diverse teams, including larger Front-End and smaller Mobile teams, to deliver high-quality solutions.

My management approach encompasses hiring, mentoring, performance management, and shaping technical roadmaps, all while fostering a collaborative and positive team culture. I thrive in consistently improving of our team's intake process for new work and collaborating closely with stakeholders to significantly streamline project definition and delivery, ensuring timely and high-quality solutions.

While my focus has shifted predominantly towards management, I maintain a strong understanding of web UI, web frameworks, and both native and hybrid mobile app development. I'm adept at full-scale application development, establishing best practices, and leveraging Agile methodologies to drive successful project delivery.

Personal Side

Beyond the world of code and management, I'm an avid enthusiast of continuous learning and personal growth. When I'm not solving technical challenges or leading teams, you'll likely find me exploring new technologies, working on personal projects, or diving into interesting technical podcasts and books.
